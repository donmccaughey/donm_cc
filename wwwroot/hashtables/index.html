Hashtables

Hashtables are such an interesting and foundational data structure, though sadly but understandably missing from the C standard library (and only added to C++ in C++ 11 ).

I have my own unpolished hashtable for C and I enjoy collecting links to other implementations, hashing functions and related articles.
