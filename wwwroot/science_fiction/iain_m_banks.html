Don McCaughey Science Fiction

Iain M Banks

Banks passed away in 2013, and I've been carefully parceling out the remaining Culture novels since. I was hooked early into Consider Phlebas, though The Player of Games and Use of Weapons are probably better and more accessible starting places. For me, Look to Windward best captures the heart and soul of the Culture.
