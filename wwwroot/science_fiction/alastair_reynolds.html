Alastair Reynolds

Reynolds is an astrophysicist turned writer with a knack for creating trippy, mind-bending stories.

Revelation Space

Revelation Space is the gateway drug that got me hooked on Reynolds' strange vision of the future. It forms a loosely connected trilogy with Redemption Ark (featuring a riveting near-light-speed chase between the stars spanning decades) and Absolution Gap (with a virus-induced religious cult, moving cathedrals and a vanishing gas giant).

Aurora Rising and Elysium Fire form the Prefect Dreyfuss Emergency sub-series. They're police procedurals set in the Glitter Band, a swarm of thousands of orbital habitats.
