Walter Jon Williams

Dread Empire's Fall

The Dread Empire's Fall series is conventional military space opera with all the tropes: space battles in a star-spanning empire dominated by noble families with far-too-clever protagonists who succeed spectacularly despite overwhelming odds against them. Williams takes us on a fun stroll down this well-trodden path.
