Business Novels

Business writing is full of story telling. Authors try to hook you with a case study or anecdote (often anonymized or fictionalized) that demonstrates how their technique or framework leads the protagonist to business success. The boring abstract discussion follows. A few authors have turned the story part up to eleven and produced full-fledged business novels. The writing is sometimes amateurish and uneven, but I find this genre particularly fun.
